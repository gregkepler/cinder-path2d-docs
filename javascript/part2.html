Part 1: Path2D Basics

A Path2D instance is constructed of points and segments.

A point refers to points where a line segment connects starts and ends as well as handles that define the curve of the path.

A segment refers to the line segment as defined by the points and handles. The types of segments are MOVETO, LINETO, QUADTO, and CUBICTO and CLOSE. Every path must start with a MOVETO segment.
